What is Hello World?

HELLO WORLD

Hello World is a global digital platform that supports types of technology discussions. Founded on 2023 by a software development student, Hanif Ibrahim Zaki, with an Indonesian nationality.

With the motto of Innovating World's Technology, Hello World has the goal to gather humans all over the world for a better technology development based by unity and social interactions. Anyone can be part of Hello World community to make world's technology better. Share ideas, experiences, and knowledge all for free.

Whether you are a gamer, developer, or an architecture, you can always be a part of technologies' inovations. Innovate the world to be a better place for the next generations. And with your help and a small try we can always change the world.

No dream too big. No try too small.
